About Me

Welcome to my website. Here you’ll get to know about my work, projects and my story. So let me start by telling something about me.

I’ve been curious about computers and technologies since I first played video games with my cousin on his Commodore 64. In an effort to learn more about computers and technologies, I joined the U.S. Army as a 29V (Microwave Systems Operator and Repairer). Unfortunately, I was unable to learn much about computers and network systems. However, I learned a lot about telecommunication systems.

After the military, I returned to school and earned a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering from the University of Washington in Seattle, WA, and a Master of Science in Systems Engineering from the George Washington University in Washington, DC. I returned to the Department of Defense as a contractor in software systems engineering.

In my spare time, I continue to learn different technologies that benefit me both at work and at school (Doctor of Engineering Program in Engineering Management). I’m very passionate in new web technologies (i.e., Cloudera Hadoop with MapReduce, Docker, and Amazon Web Services) and try to implement them in my projects. I’m also interested in data science (i.e., Python and R), and machine learning. Browse my website to know more about me. Thank you.
